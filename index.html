    • Project Description

    Chef Masters is a site all about cooking.

    Have you ever struggled with navigating through the endless blog sections on other cooking websites?

    Have you ever wished you could just get straight down to recipe without a horde of ads slowing down your device?

    With Chef Masters, we focus on the essentials, eliminating extraneous information and providing a streamlined recipe sharing experience. Our website allows users to create, post, edit, and rate recipes at will. Advanced search functionalities let users seach by rating, views, categories, and more. Enjoy this streamlined approach to recipe sharing that will leave you breathless!
